## GV — Govern {#govern}

| Category | Subcategory Examples | Typical Evidence |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| GV.OC — Organizational Context | Understand mission, stakeholder expectations, legal/regulatory requirements | Mission statement, compliance register, stakeholder analysis |
| GV.RM — Risk Management Strategy | Risk management strategy established and communicated | Risk management policy, risk appetite statement |
| GV.RR — Roles, Responsibilities & Authorities | Cybersecurity roles established and communicated | RACI matrix, org chart, job descriptions |
| GV.PO — Policy | Organizational cybersecurity policy established | Information security policy, policy framework |
| GV.OV — Oversight | Governance and risk management oversight activities | Board meeting minutes, management reviews |
| GV.SC — Supply Chain Risk Management | Cyber supply chain risk management activities | Vendor risk policy, TPRM program, supply chain assessments |

## ID — Identify {#identify}

| Category | Subcategory Examples | Typical Evidence |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| ID.AM — Asset Management | Inventories of hardware, software, services, data flows maintained | Asset inventory, CMDB, data flow diagrams |
| ID.RA — Risk Assessment | Threats, vulnerabilities, likelihood, and impact identified and documented | Risk assessments, threat modeling, vulnerability scans |
| ID.IM — Improvement | Improvements identified from evaluations, exercises, assessments | Lessons learned, improvement plans, audit findings |

## PR — Protect {#protect}

| Category | Subcategory Examples | Typical Evidence |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| PR.AA — Identity Management, Authentication, and Access Control | Identities, credentials, and access managed | IAM policies, MFA configuration, access reviews |
| PR.AT — Awareness and Training | Personnel are provided cybersecurity awareness and training | Training program, completion records, phishing simulations |
| PR.DS — Data Security | Data is managed consistent with risk strategy | Encryption policy, DLP configuration, classification scheme |
| PR.PS — Platform Security | Hardware, software, and services managed consistent with risk strategy | Hardening standards, patch management, endpoint protection |
| PR.IR — Technology Infrastructure Resilience | Security architectures managed to protect asset confidentiality, integrity, availability | Architecture reviews, redundancy designs, network segmentation |

## DE — Detect {#detect}

| Category | Subcategory Examples | Typical Evidence |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| DE.CM — Continuous Monitoring | Assets monitored to find anomalies, IoCs, and other events | SIEM configuration, monitoring procedures, alert rules |
| DE.AE — Adverse Event Analysis | Anomalies and events analyzed to characterize potential adverse events | Event correlation rules, analysis procedures, triage documentation |

## RS — Respond {#respond}

| Category | Subcategory Examples | Typical Evidence |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| RS.MA — Incident Management | Responses to detected incidents are managed | Incident response plan, IR team roster, escalation procedures |
| RS.AN — Incident Analysis | Investigation conducted to determine incident scope and root cause | Forensic procedures, root cause analysis templates |
| RS.CO — Incident Response Reporting and Communication | Response activities coordinated with stakeholders | Communication plan, notification procedures, stakeholder lists |
| RS.MI — Incident Mitigation | Activities performed to prevent expansion and mitigate effects | Containment procedures, eradication checklists |

## RC — Recover {#recover}

| Category | Subcategory Examples | Typical Evidence |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| RC.RP — Incident Recovery Plan Execution | Recovery activities performed | DR plans, recovery procedures, restore runbooks |
| RC.CO — Incident Recovery Communication | Recovery progress communicated to stakeholders | Status update templates, communication logs |
